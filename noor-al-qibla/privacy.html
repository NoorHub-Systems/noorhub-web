Introduction

Welcome to Noor Al Qibla ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how our mobile application ("App") collects, uses, and protects your information.

Information We Collect

1. Location Data

What we collect: Your device's GPS coordinates and location information.

How we use it: Location data is used exclusively on your device to:

  • Calculate the Qibla direction pointing to Makkah
  • Determine accurate prayer times for your location
  • Display your current city and coordinates
Important: Your location data is processed locally on your device and is NOT sent to our servers or stored externally. We do not track your movements or share your location with third parties.

2. Device Permissions

Our App requires the following permissions:

  • Location Access: Required to calculate Qibla direction and prayer times
  • Vibration: Used to provide haptic feedback when aligned to Qibla
  • Notifications: Used to alert you of prayer times (optional, only if you enable it)
  • Internet Access: Required to display advertisements

3. Local Storage

The App stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Language preferences
  • Prayer time notification settings
  • Last known location for faster calculations

This data remains on your device and is not transmitted to any servers.

Third-Party Services

Google AdMob

Our App displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and use data for advertising purposes, including:

  • Device information (model, operating system)
  • IP address
  • Advertising ID
  • Usage data and interactions with ads

AdMob's data collection is governed by Google's Privacy Policy:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

You can opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device settings:

  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization
  • iOS: Settings → Privacy → Advertising → Limit Ad Tracking

Google Play Services

Our App uses Google Play Services for in-app purchases and location services. These services are subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

Data We Do NOT Collect

We do NOT collect, store, or transmit:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • User accounts or login credentials
  • Payment information (handled by Google Play Store)
  • Browsing history or app usage analytics
  • Your precise location history or movement patterns

Children's Privacy

Our App does not knowingly collect information from children under the age of 13. The App is designed for general audiences and does not target children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at noor.islamic.apps@gmail.com.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information:

  • Location data is processed locally and not transmitted
  • Local storage uses device-level encryption
  • We do not maintain databases with user information
  • No user accounts means no account security risks

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: View what data is stored locally on your device
  • Delete: Uninstall the App to remove all locally stored data
  • Control: Disable location permissions or notifications in device settings
  • Opt-out: Disable personalized ads through your device settings

Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

For users in the European Union, our legal basis for processing your data is:

  • Legitimate Interest: To provide core app functionality (Qibla direction, prayer times)
  • Consent: For optional features like notifications and personalized ads

Data Retention

Since we do not collect or store user data on our servers:

  • All data is stored locally on your device
  • Data is retained until you uninstall the App or clear app data
  • No data retention policies are needed for server-side storage

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:
