About the App

Noor Al Duaa is a spiritual companion bringing together supplications (duaas), Ziyarat, and daily Islamic remembrances in one place. From the recommended Duaa of the Day to the full Sahifa Sajjadiyya, listen to beautifully recited audio, read along, and keep your favorites close at hand - anytime, anywhere.
