Introduction

Welcome to Noor Al Duaa ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application ("App") — a companion for supplications (duaas), Ziyarat, and daily Islamic remembrances — collects, uses, and protects your information.

Information We Collect

1. Audio Content Delivery

What we use: Recitation audio files for duaas are streamed or downloaded from Google Firebase Storage on demand, and cached on your device for offline playback.

How it works: The App requests audio files by filename/category directly from our Firebase Storage bucket. No personal or account information is attached to these requests beyond standard network information (such as your IP address) that is inherent to any internet connection.

Important: We do not require an account to use the App, and we do not track which specific duaas you listen to, read, or download on any server we control.

2. Device Permissions

Our App requests the following permissions:

  • Internet Access: Required to stream/download duaa audio and display advertisements
  • Notifications: Used to send you a daily duaa reminder (optional, only if you enable it)
  • Exact Alarms / Boot Completed: Used so your scheduled daily reminder still fires at the correct time after your device restarts

The App does not request location, camera, microphone, or contacts permissions.

3. Local Storage

The App stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Favorite duaas you have bookmarked
  • Downloaded audio files (for offline playback)
  • Hijri calendar date-offset preference
  • Notification/reminder time and enabled/disabled preference
  • Language and theme (light/dark) preferences
  • Premium (ad-free) purchase status

This data remains on your device and is not transmitted to any servers we control.

Third-Party Services

Google AdMob

Our App displays advertisements (banner and interstitial ads) provided by Google AdMob, unless you have purchased the ad-free premium upgrade. AdMob may collect and use data for advertising purposes, including:

  • Device information (model, operating system)
  • IP address
  • Advertising ID
  • Usage data and interactions with ads

AdMob's data collection is governed by Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

You can opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device settings:

  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization
  • iOS: Settings → Privacy → Advertising → Limit Ad Tracking
Managing or revoking ad consent (EEA, UK, Switzerland, and certain U.S. states): If you are located in the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom, Switzerland, or a U.S. state with applicable privacy legislation, the App presents a consent message the first time it detects you may be in one of these regions, letting you choose whether to allow personalized advertising. You can review, change, or revoke that consent choice at any time from within the App: open Settings → Manage Ad Consent. This will re-present the consent options so you can update your choice whenever you like.

Google Firebase

The App uses Firebase Storage to host and deliver audio recitation files, and Firebase Core for basic app initialization. These services are subject to Google's Privacy Policy and do not collect personally identifiable information as used in this App.

Google Play Services

The App uses Google Play Services for in-app purchases (the ad-free premium upgrade). This service is subject to Google's Privacy Policy, and payment information is handled entirely by the Google Play Store — we never see or store your payment details.

Data We Do NOT Collect

We do NOT collect, store, or transmit:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • User accounts or login credentials
  • Payment information (handled by Google Play Store)
  • Your device's location
  • Browsing history or app usage analytics

Children's Privacy

Our App does not knowingly collect information from children under the age of 13. The App is designed for general audiences and does not target children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at admin@noorhubsystems.com.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information:

  • App preferences and favorites are stored locally using device-level encryption
  • Downloaded audio files are stored in the App's private storage area
  • We do not maintain servers or databases containing personal user information
  • No user accounts means no account security risks

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: View what data is stored locally on your device
  • Delete: Uninstall the App, or clear its downloaded audio/favorites, to remove all locally stored data
  • Control: Disable notification permissions in device settings
  • Opt-out: Disable personalized ads through your device settings, or through Settings → Manage Ad Consent in the App

Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Posting a notification in the App (for material changes)

Your continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

For users in the European Union, our legal basis for processing your data is:

  • Legitimate Interest: To provide core app functionality (delivering duaa audio/text content)
  • Consent: For optional features like notifications and personalized ads

Users in the EEA, UK, and Switzerland may withdraw consent to personalized advertising at any time via Settings → Manage Ad Consent in the App, as described above.

Data Retention

Since we do not collect or store user data on our servers:

  • All preference data is stored locally on your device
  • Data is retained until you uninstall the App or clear app data
  • No data retention policies are needed for server-side storage

Your California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information is collected
  • Request deletion of personal information
  • Opt-out of the sale of personal information

Note: We do not sell personal information. All App preferences stay on your device.
